Making Tea

1.Take 100ml of water as solvent and boil it for few minutes.

2.When solvent boils, add solutes such as milk, tea leaves, sugar.

3.Now again boil the solution for few minutes.

4.Sugar is soluble in water thus it will be dissolve.

5.Colour of tea leaves goes into solution as filtrate.

Making Green Tea

4.Stir the tea once, and then let it steep for 1.5-2 minutes.
